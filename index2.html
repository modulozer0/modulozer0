The mission

Our mission is to teach the basics of science and engineering. We want to develop professionally, as our motto "We define impossible" also supports. We want to participate in the modernization of the country and the development of the community.

The First
Concept

The FIRST concept means more than building a robot, it is the desire to learn new and important things such as: entrepreneurial skills, expression, teamwork and many others, all fundamental to most professions of the future. We intend to we cultivate curiosity and spark the interest of new generations in robotics. We wish that future enlightened minds to contribute to the development of the community and become even our team members. We support the program, we want to raise the bar with the goal of a continuously improve ourselves not only as individuals but also as a united team. We have mastered it the "gracious professionalism" principle, a spirit we want to pass on to others teams.
