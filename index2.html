Who are we?

We are Modulo Zero, the robotics team that belongs to the Maramureș County Center of Excellence and that participates in the First Tech Challenge competition within the FIRST Inspires and Nation through Education program.

Vision & Mision

Our FTC team is dedicated to bringing these values ​​to life through innovation, teamwork and excellence. We strive to motivate and educate young people to become leaders in the technology sector, helping them develop their "problem solving" skills and promoting a culture of teamwork and fair play. Our passion for robotics and STEM inspires us to make positive changes in our community and prepare for the challenges of the future. With every project, we are determined to add a touch of creativity and exceed expectations, applying our knowledge to spark curiosity about our field.

What makes us special?

Our passion for technology motivated us to establish one of the only robotics teams in Romania that does not depend on a school. Thus, we give a chance to dozens of children who share the same calling as us, but do not belong to a school that has a robotics club.

Conceptul
First

The First Tech Challenge, also known as the FTC, is a robotics competition for students in grades 7-12 where participants must design, build and program a robot to compete against other teams. This competition aims to promote the values ​​of STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics) education among young people.
